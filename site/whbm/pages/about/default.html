Who Says You Can’t Have It All...

It’s Real Fashion, but Crafted Like Couture

From sketch to prototype to final product, we craft our pieces with the same approach as the world’s design houses. Our onsite design studios give us the edge—there we create curated collections in the highest quality fabrics with intricate details and a tailor-made feel. The only thing missing is the designer price tag.

It’s Putting Together Your Signature Style

Work, weekend, whatever—each piece in our collection is designed with versatility in mind. Our expert stylists will show you how to get more mileage from everything you wear. Plus they’re always here to help with not only transforming your closet and tracking down that one elusive item, but also being available before or after working hours because we know that more time is priceless.

It’s a Fit that's Far Superior to our Competitors

We take fit seriously. Our collections undergo a series of rigorous tests to ensure the fabric fits real women’s bodies. Each piece is worn, washed and tweaked to perfection—from seam to hem to hardware—no detail is overlooked. The end result is clothing that moves effortlessly with women’s on-the-go, multifaceted lives.

It’s a Price that Surprises in a Good Way

We believe you shouldn’t have to sacrifice quality for price. Value is a given with everything we do. Case in point, 90% of our collection is machine washable. It’s the price we pay to give you what you want…without the sticker shock.

Our History

We began as two small Baltimore, Maryland neighborhood boutiques

The White House, opened in 1985, and Black Market, opened in 1995—both founded for and run by women. The vision for each was to be a special, personal style destination for women everywhere.

In 1997, the two stores became one and White House Black Market was born. We’ve grown since then, but women and their on-the-go, multifaceted lives continue to inspire us. We’re dedicated to redefining luxury every day with elevated style and quality, exceptional service, perfect fit and great price.

1985

The White House opens in Baltimore‘s Harborplace mall, featuring apparel and accessories in purest white.

1995

Black Market debuts with an extended focus on offering fashion-forward collections in shades of black.

1997

The White House and Black Market and their concepts were combined into one boutique.

2003

Chico’s FAS acquires White House Black Market and the main office is relocated to Fort Myers, FL.

2004

Living Beyond Breast Cancer begins their partnership with White House Black Market.

2005

Black Book (now WHBM Rewards), a rewards program celebrating our customers, is launched.

2008

First lady Michelle Obama wears a WHBM dress on The View. Virtually overnight, the dress sells out.

2009

The “Feel Beautiful” campaign launches nationally and we land the cover of WWD.

2010

Dress for Success SWF is co-founded by WHBM. We have supported the organization with donations of $10K annually.

2012

Coco Rocha is featured in our commerical wearing a dozen outfits from the company’s WorkKit collection.

2013

Perfect Form, a WHBM exclusive fabric is launched.

2014

International Boutiques. We expand into Canada with a location in Toronto.

2016

White House Black Market evolves to connect with the customer. New logo and new branding.

 

The first mailer with our new logo was Spring 2016 and the first store that opened with the new branding was in Las Vegas.
